My Favorite TV Show & Movie

Attack on Titan

This show features one of the best plots in anime history, masterfully crafted by
Hajime Isayama. The series initially began as a show about humankind fighting
titans. However, as the story progressed, themes such as violence, discrimination,
politics, and freedom emerged within a much deeper, more complex, and twisted plot.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

This film, released in 2021, created lots of nostalgia for us young adults by bringing all
3 spider mans from different dimensions into one movie.

  • Tobey Maguire (left)
  • Tom Holland (middle)
  • Andrew Garfield (right)

Its simple. They took the best things of all of the Spider-Man films and packed them
together neatly. No unnecessary filler, no overexplanation. Just put the pieces thereand
let the players do their thing.

Moral/Theme

Before making major decisions, think things through, write down your options,
consider your options, discuss the with other people and consider the consequences.
Plan things carefully so that both you and anyone helping you know all the facts before
taking any drastic action.
